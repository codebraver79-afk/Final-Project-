This project displays a movies website where you can check movies and their details, search for movies and save movies.

Technologies used: HTML, CSS and JS

How to use the website:
Click on the search bar enter any movie title and click on enter to see the movies you searched for. Click on the favorites icon and save icon at the bottom of each movie card to save them to the Favorites or Watchlist section. Click on Browse movies to go back to hope page  Click on the card image to check the movie's details like release date, star rating, overview, cast, reviews. Click on the 'watch trailer' to watch the movie's trailer form youtube. Click on add Review button to add your own review by entering your name and your feedback. You can add a star rating. After clicking on submit, this review will be saved below the other reviews (if there are any).You can click on the X top right to cancel your review. You can check for the recommended movies at the bottom of each movie you click. You can click on the X top right to go back to the home page.
